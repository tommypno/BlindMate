Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22.11.2016

The service provided by BlindMate is regulated by the Norwegian law about personal information (https://lovdata.no/lov/2000-04-14-31) and the Norwegian law concerning electronic communication (https://lovdata.no/lov/2003-07-04-83). These laws determine what type of information that could be collected by BlindMate and how this information is used.

The judicial regulations make sure of correct treatment of personal information. This includes such as the right to know what personal information is stored by BlindMate.


Information that is collected and why it is collected

BlindMate collects information about:

  • data entered in the application by the user

Collected to create travel suggestions adapted for users who are blind or have low vision. The information is also used to create and save favorite travel routes.

  • the location of the user when this is allowed

Collected to switch automatically to navigation for walking when the user exits the bus/train/tram.

  • the smart phone’s language settings

To change the language in the application based on the language used on the smart phone.


Third parties

Because BlindMate is integrated with services from Ruter and Google. That is why information that is collected by Ruter and Google regulated by their personal privacy disclaimers. These are available from:

Ruter: https://ruter.no/en/getting-help/terms-and-conditions/personal-data- protection/ruterbillett-and-ruterreise/

Google: https://maps.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/

The Norwegian law about personal information ensures that BlindMate will not distribute personal information to third parties without the user’s acceptance. The personal information that is stored by BlindMate will only be used for the purpose of the service.
